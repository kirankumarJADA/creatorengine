Privacy Policy

CreatorEngine · Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Jada Kiran Kumar ("we", "us"), operator of CreatorEngine (the "Service"), collects, uses, and protects your information. CreatorEngine helps Instagram professional accounts automatically reply to comments and messages. By using the Service you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account data: the email address and password (stored hashed) you use to create a CreatorEngine account.
  • Instagram data (via Meta): when you connect your Instagram professional account, we access your profile (username, account ID, profile picture), your media/posts, comments on your media, direct messages related to your account, and an access token — used only to provide the automation features you set up.
  • Automation data: the automations, keywords, messages, and contacts you create in the Service.
  • Usage logs: activity and error logs needed to operate and debug the Service.

How we use your information

  • To detect comments and messages on your connected account and send the automated replies you configure.
  • To display your account, posts, automations, and activity in your dashboard.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and operate the Service.

We do not sell your personal data or your Instagram data, and we do not use it for advertising.

How your data is stored and shared

Data is stored using Google Firebase / Firestore and processed on our hosting provider (Render). Access tokens are stored securely and never exposed publicly. We share data only with these processors strictly to run the Service: Meta Platforms / Instagram (to access the Instagram data you authorize), Google Firebase (storage), and our hosting provider. Our use of Instagram data complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

Data deletion

You can remove your data at any time:

  • Disconnect Instagram: use "Disconnect" in the Service to revoke our access and delete your stored Instagram data and token.
  • Delete your account: email us at jadakirank@gmail.com from your account email and we will delete your account and associated data within 30 days.
  • You may also remove CreatorEngine from your Instagram account settings under Apps and Websites; we treat that deauthorization as a request to delete the associated data.

Data retention

We keep your data only while your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. After deletion or disconnection, we remove the associated data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Children

The Service is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy and will revise the "Last updated" date above when we do.

Contact
